Our Story

Cooked with Love & Tradition

Since 2012, Spice Garden has been serving authentic flavours of India in the heart of Chennai. Every dish is prepared fresh daily using traditional spices sourced directly from local farmers.

Our head chef, trained in Hyderabad and Lucknow, brings decades of expertise to your plate. We believe food is more than just sustenance — it's a story, a memory, an emotion.
